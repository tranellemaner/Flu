Last season's flu was the worst on record for children?

True or False

True

Except for the swine flu epidemic in 2009-2010, the record for largest number of reported flu deaths in children was held by the single season of 2012-2013 with about 171 pediatric deaths. This past year’s strain of flu was severe not only for the general public but more so for children than any other year in the past. The number of pediatric deaths reached a new high of 179 as of August 2018.

Avoiding sick people is the best way for people to stay healthy?

True or False

False

The best way to remain healthy is to get vaccinated. For both the 2016-2017 and 2017-2018 seasons the ACIP recommended that LAIV4 or Live attenuated influenza vaccine not be distributed because of efficacy. The ACIP analyzed data from 2010-11 through 2016-17 and found that children were the least protected without this vaccination method.

Conclusion:

Conclusion

According to ControlFlu.com during 2016-17, the first year when the FluMist was not available, there was a 41% decrease in the overall vaccination rate across public schools. It’s possible that the decrease in the vaccination number led to more students contracting the disease; however, there is no concrete data record of children who contract the flu.
